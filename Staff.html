Laura Sherman

Office Manager/Billing

Laura was born and raised in Twin Falls. She graduated from Twin Falls High School and received a secretarial degree from the College of Southern Idaho. She has been married to her husband, Scott, for 33 years and has 2 children, Matt and Jenni and a son-in-law, Austin. They have 3 dogs at home now and 2 grandpups. She has been the office manager/legal assistant for the past 19 years with Stephan, Kvanvig, Stone & Trainor and enjoys working for Russ and Laird and working with her staff.

When not at the office, she enjoys camping, 4-wheeling, snowmobiling, boating, kayaking, traveling, hunting and spending lots of time with her family and her friends. Laura is the family photographer and loves taking pictures to look back on. She enjoys watching softball and baseball and loves when the family goes to Seattle to watch the Mariners play.

Laura is responsible for the day-to-day management of the firm, including billing.
